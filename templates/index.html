About Us

Fact-Check AI is the first Algerian platform powered by artificial intelligence, specialized in detecting fake news and analyzing content credibility. We provide a powerful tool to combat misinformation and raise awareness about the importance of accessing reliable information. Our platform aims to support the Algerian and Arab communities with advanced technological solutions to ensure the circulation of accurate news and to build a more informed and trustworthy digital society.
